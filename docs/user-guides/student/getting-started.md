## Logging In

To log in to the EdPsych AI Education Platform:

1. Go to the platform website or open the app
2. Click on "Student Login"
3. Enter your username and password provided by your school
4. Click "Log In"

If you've forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset it.

## Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalised dashboard:

![Student Dashboard](../images/student-dashboard.png)

Your dashboard includes:

1. **Learning Path**: Your personalised learning journey
2. **Upcoming Assessments**: Tests and assignments due soon
3. **Recent Progress**: Your recent achievements and progress
4. **Messages**: Communications from your teachers
5. **Recommended Activities**: Activities suggested based on your learning style and progress

Use the navigation menu on the left to access different sections of the platform.

## Your Learning Path

Your learning path is personalised to your needs, learning style, and pace:

1. Click on "My Learning Path" in the navigation menu
2. View your current modules and progress
3. Click on a module to begin or continue learning
4. Complete activities in the recommended order for best results

Each module includes:
- Learning objectives
- Interactive content
- Practice activities
- Assessments

Your progress is automatically saved, so you can continue where you left off.

## Completing Assessments

Assessments help measure your understanding and progress:

1. Access assessments from your dashboard or learning path
2. Read the instructions carefully before starting
3. Complete all questions within the time limit (if applicable)
4. Submit your assessment when finished
5. Review your results and feedback

Types of assessments include:
- Multiple choice questions
- Short answer questions
- Essay questions
- Interactive activities
- Project submissions

## Accessibility Features

The platform includes several accessibility features to support your learning:

1. **Text-to-Speech**: Have text read aloud to you
2. **Font Adjustments**: Change font size and style
3. **Colour Schemes**: Choose high-contrast or colour-blind friendly modes
4. **Keyboard Navigation**: Navigate without a mouse
5. **Screen Reader Support**: Compatible with popular screen readers

To access these features:
1. Click on your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Accessibility Settings"
3. Choose the options that work best for you

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. Click on the "Help" icon (?) in the top right corner
2. Browse the FAQ section for common questions
3. Use the search bar to find specific topics
4. Contact your teacher through the messaging system
5. For technical issues, click "Report a Problem"

Remember, your teachers and the support team are here to help you succeed!
